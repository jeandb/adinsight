## About the Business

Prof Jaque Mendes is a digital influencer and entrepreneur in the pedagogy
niche in Brazil. She operates an ecosystem of four main products:

### Active Products

**Loja das Profs** (`lojadasprofs.com.br`)
- WooCommerce store selling pedagogical infoproducts as downloadable PDFs
- Products: classroom activities, lesson plans, ready-to-use teaching resources
- Audience: teachers looking for practical, ready-made classroom materials
- Model: individual product purchase
- Status: gradually migrating to the Tudo de Prof marketplace

**Clube das Profs** (`clubedasprofs.com.br`)
- Annual subscription (WooCommerce + YITH Subscription & Membership)
- Grants access to the full Loja das Profs catalog for 1 year
- Value proposition: savings vs. individual purchases + unlimited access
- Key metrics: active subscribers, churn, MRR, renewals

**Tudo de Prof** (`tudodeprof.com.br`)
- Pedagogical resources marketplace (WooCommerce + WCFM)
- Prof Jaque is both the marketplace administrator AND a seller
- External seller plans: Free (25% commission), Professional (20%), Unlimited (15%)
- Mentoria students: free marketplace access with 15% commission
- Key metrics: total GMV, Prof Jaque's own sales, seller growth

**Mentoria Do Giz ao Digital**
- Training program teaching teachers how to create and sell digital pedagogical resources
- Offers Tudo de Prof marketplace access as a core benefit
- No payment integration mapped in the system yet (future phase)
- Metrics: unavailable at this time

## Target Audience

**Primary profile:** Brazilian elementary and middle school teachers
- Age range: 25–45 years old
- Income: middle class, looking to save time and earn supplemental income
- Pain points: no time to create materials from scratch, desire to stand out professionally
- Aspirations: practicality, professional recognition, income from digital products
- Digital behavior: active on Instagram and TikTok, consume pedagogical content regularly

**Secondary profile:** Teachers transitioning to digital entrepreneurship
- Interested in selling their own resources online
- Looking for guidance on how to monetize their knowledge
- Willing to invest in mentorship programs

---

## Brand Voice

- **Welcoming (Acolhedor):** speaks to teachers as a colleague, not as a vendor
- **Practical (Prático):** focus on concrete solutions, no fluff
- **Inspiring (Inspirador):** celebrates achievements, encourages beginners
- **Authentic (Autêntico):** conversational language, no corporate jargon
- **Empowering (Empoderador):** reinforces the teacher's value and potential

## Pedagogical Niche Benchmarks

Use as reference when evaluating whether a metric is performing well, average, or poorly:

| Metric | Niche reference |
|---|---|
| CTR (Meta) | Good: > 1.5% \| Average: 0.8–1.5% \| Poor: < 0.8% |
| CTR (Google Search) | Good: > 5% \| Average: 2–5% \| Poor: < 2% |
| CPL (lead generation) | Good: < R$8 \| Average: R$8–18 \| Poor: > R$18 |
| ROAS (infoproducts) | Good: > 4x \| Average: 2–4x \| Poor: < 2x |
| Landing page conversion rate | Good: > 3% \| Average: 1–3% \| Poor: < 1% |
| Ad frequency (Meta) | Ideal: 1.5–3x \| Saturation: > 4x |

> Note: benchmarks are references, not absolute targets. Always contextualize with campaign objective and funnel stage.
